About the estate

Cyberia Virtual real-estate is the first of many purchasable areas within Cyberia. We are proud to present the first ever condominiums for sale within Cyberia, ranging in size from small, medium, large and penthouse suites!

The exact release date will be announced soon as we finish up development. The price will be decided with input from the community to ensure fair pricing. Prices will be fixed and apartment NFT's will be sent out randomly between the available sizes!

Upon purchase, you will have access to your unfurnished condo within Cyberia. Inside you will find various pieces of furniture available to you in order to customize your condo to your liking! In the future these furniture pieces will cost $CYPX but for now they will be free for all testers!
